We'll help you to plan your Wedding

CloudBerry Hotel employees the experienced wedding planers who will help you in every step of the way to make your dream wedding come true. When you contact Cloudberry you will be assigned a wedding planer and until the time bride and groom leave the hotel he'll make sure everything runs smoothly. He can help you with selecting and customising the wedding package to fit your requirements. Every wedding planing team consist of 7 staff members, so you can be rest assured we will be taking care of any contingents for you.

A Luxury Ballroom

Our Grand Ballrooms are the perfect venue for your dream wedding. With its elegant décor and stunning views, it is the perfect place to celebrate your special day. We have two ballrooms and each one caters to different number of guests. One of the ballroom can accommodate up to 200 guests, and the other one can accommodate 100 guests. Both Ballrooms feature a dance floor, stage, and sound system. The sound systems and lightnings are the latest technology to make sure everything goes perfect.

We Create The Most Beautiful Wedding Cakes

For couple to cut the cake and share the first bites and to serve it to their guests the cake needs to be perfect. That's why Cloudberry has world's most famous chefs creating wedding cakes for you. You will be blown away not only with the design of cake but with the taste as well. We use the finest ingredients to bake the cakes. When you book a wedding with us, you will be served free samples of types of cake we provide at weddings. So you can choose the one that you love. If you have a design in mind you can go with that too.

You Get to Choose How The Menu Should Be

The most waited part when it comes to a wedding is the meal time. At Cloudberry, you can choose the cuisine you like and the menu items that come with that cuisine. So if you want you and your guests to enjoy an italian style cuisine or chinese or even French now you have the option to do so. And if you like to have just few items from another cuisine added your main cuisine you can do that as well. Our catering options comes with range of packages to fit any kind of budget. Also, our food is made by world-famous chefs, so you can be rest assured you will dine on the most amazing food at your wedding.
