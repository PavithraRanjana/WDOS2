Cloudberry Hotels Has Three Luxury Restaurants


We have three Restaurants each led by a world-famous chef. And each of the restaurant specialises in range of cuisines from around the world. Our restaurants are designed in a way that brings you the most luxurious dining experiance available in the world. With range of authentic cuisines you will feel like you are traveling to each country those cuisines belongs to.


Each of our three restaurants operates 24/7 hours a day, and we offer in room dining 24/7 hours as well. So you can indulge in finest dining whenever you want.


Dine Like a Frenchman at Versailles Restaurant


French food is very famous for it's favour and elegant presentations. The French cuisine includes hundreds of high quality wines and cheese. Most of the French dishes are created using simple recipes but with high quality ingredients. Another special thing about the French cuisine is that they mostly prefer meat in their dishes.


At Cloudberry you can find the most authentic french dishes made by french national chefs. Our French dishes are made using most fresh ingredients just like they do in France. Each dish comes with a range of high quality wines.

Explore The Most Authentic Italian Cuisine


Italian cuisine is known all over the world as one of the best cuisines in the world. Italian food has been adopted by almost every country in the world to the point that each country has modified the dishes to fit their own culture. Food such as Pizza, Lasagne and Pasta represent italian culinary excellence all over the world


At Cloudberry you can taste the Lasagne and Pasta and many more Italian food just as they would have made in Itly. The colosseum Italian restaurant is lead by an Italian national chef. Cloudberry Hotels imports finest red wine from Italy which you can have with a dish of Lasagne or pasta. At our Italian restaurant you indulge a fully Italian Culinary experience.


Experience the chinese cuisine at Emperor's Restaurant


chinese cuisine is famous all over the world for being very distinctive from all the other cuisine in the world. Many chinese dishes are given a sweet, sour and yet a spicy flavour. chinese Dumplings, Fried Rice, Spring Rolls are world-famous.

At Cloudberry Hotel's the Emperor's Restaurant is lead by a chinese national chef. So you can have the most authentic chinese food. You can request to have your meal prepared at your table. Then you can see first-hand why chinese cuisine is a form of art.
